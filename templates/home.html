Welcome to the RNE Quality Check framework. You are entering the last frontier that AI can only aspire to conquer, where your expertise and communication skills can make a huge difference. Bug Release-note (RNE) enables our customers to determine if the Cisco network, product, or service is subject to a problem, identify the appropriate bug if the behaviour is observed, and provide a workaround to the problem if possible. Your contribution to having good RNEs will have a direct correlation to a lesser number of customer SRs.

Release-note is a living document, it is updated whenever useful information about symptoms, conditions and workarounds becomes available. RNE compliance check is required per CPDM. Our BU goal is to review all bugs that exist in shipped code upon bug resolution.

Find the RNE TOI videos and lots of other great RNE resources at: RNE-Know it All
